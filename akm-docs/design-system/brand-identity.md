# AI Krushi Mitra — Brand Identity & Tone of Voice

> **Version:** 1.0 | **Status:** Approved | **Owner:** Creative Director  
> **Last Updated:** 2026-06-28

---

## 1. Visual Assets

*   **Logo Lockup:** A clean geometric leaf node embedded inside a digital circuit grid, representing the connection of nature and AI.
*   **Colors:** Primary Forest Green (`#10b981`) represents crops and agriculture; Accent Soil Amber (`#f59e0b`) represents harvest prosperity.
*   **Default Typography:** Outfit / Inter. Clean sans-serif fonts optimized for mobile layout scaling.

---

## 2. Tone & Voice Guidelines

*   **Respectful & Empathetic:** Always address the user as "शेतकरी मित्र" (Farmer Friend).
*   **No Technical Jargon:** Never use deep computer science or plant pathology terms without local colloquial explanations. For example:
    *   *Bad:* "This is a Pseudocercospora leaf spot fungal mutation."
    *   *Good:* "शेतकरी मित्रानो, तुमच्या पिकावर तांबेरा रोगाचा प्रादुर्भाव झालाय. हा बुरशीजन्य आजार आहे."
*   **Grounded & Local:** Focus on local organic solutions (neem oil sprays, balanced compost) alongside chemical remedies.

## 2. Input Brand Partnerships (Ad/Lead Gen Rules)

To maintain absolute trust within the farming community, advertising is strictly regulated under the following rules:

1.  **Contextual Only:** Ads are never displayed as random pop-ups. They are shown strictly inside relevant advice (e.g., if RAG diagnoses "Iron Deficiency" in a crop, a sponsored local link is shown for "Chelated Iron 12% fertilizer" from registered local dealers).
2.  **Unbiased AI:** The AI model prompts NEVER mention brand names directly. The brand recommendations are injected strictly as secondary links below the AI's primary active ingredients suggestion.
3.  **Local Partners First:** Priorities are given to local FPOs and village input shops to keep transport costs low for the farmer.
